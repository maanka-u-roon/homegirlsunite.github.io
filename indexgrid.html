Our Mission

Here to support, love, empower and listen to eldest immigrant daughters.

Home Girls Unite is safe space for eldest immigrant daughters, both on and offline. We provide group therapy and one to one support for immigrant women who are facing racism and discrimination, social and economic inequalities, mental health stigma.
